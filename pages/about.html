Meet our Executive Board

Our executive board is made up of passionate individuals dedicated to advancing the mission of SHPE at CMU. Each member brings unique skills and perspectives to the table, working collaboratively to create a supportive and empowering environment for all members.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to empower the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support, and development. We envision a world where Hispanics are leaders in STEM fields, driving innovation and creating positive change in their communities and beyond.

Striving for academic and social excellence while promoting professional development
